Sources & assumptions: Engagement: Gallup State of the Global Workplace 2025 — only 21% of employees globally are engaged; disengagement costs ~18% of salary in lost productivity; 70% of team engagement variance is attributable to the manager. This model claims only 10% of disengaged employees improve. Retention: Gallup 2025 found 42% of exits are preventable; we use a conservative 10% reduction. Replacement cost: SHRM 2025 — 50–200% of annual salary (model defaults to 50%). Manager effectiveness: Gallup 2025 links manager engagement directly to $438B in lost global productivity; model uses only 1% team output lift across avg team of 7. All assumptions are intentionally conservative — actual outcomes with sustained behavior change typically exceed these estimates.
